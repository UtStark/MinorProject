Guru Tegh Bahadur Institute of Technology is a private engineering college affiliated to Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University, Delhi. It was established in 1999 by the Delhi Sikh Gurudwara Management Committee. GTBIT is a degree level technical institute, approved by AICTE. Guru Tegh Bahadur Institute of Technology (GTBIT) aims to be among the premier institutes of engineering and technology in the country, recognised excellence in Teaching, R&D, Sports, Cultural and Social Arena.
